About LINKUPWITH.ME

LinkUpWith.Me was conceived and founded by Adam Gerard and Munam Wasi, two college students who wanted a better alternative to what currently existed for recruitment.

LinkUpWith.Me (www.linkupwith.me) is a multi-platform recruitment and event planning tool designed to connect anyone and everyone, while leveraging powerful, professional grade tools with analytical data for the various needs of Students, Recruiters, Businesses, Organizations, and Consumers at large. We utilize existing social networks (Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn) with plans to incorporate more as we expand and tailor our services for specific markets.

While our ambitions are global, our focus right now is local. We want to tailor our services for our colleges, friends, and immediate job opportunities, before chasing bigger goals.

On this website, you should be able to use a demo version of our application, see some commonly asked questions about the application, and see more information that will be useful for both students and recruiters. Feel free to reach out to us using the information below with any questions.

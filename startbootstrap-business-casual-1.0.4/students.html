For Students

Students will have access to a deck of events in their area and can RSVP with a click or a flick!

Students face an abundance of technical and design issues when trying to connect with recruiters that shouldn’t exist in the 21st century. Most college’s Career Centers use services like Simplicity, which have outdated, clunky User Interfaces that don’t allow for fluid interaction between student and recruiter.

Students are often forced to make multiple profiles across a variety of services, that don’t communicate with each other, and are very redundant. LinkUpWith.Me integrates existing services, so with one touch, students can link their Facebook, Twitter, and LinkedIn accounts, among other services, with their resumes, and answer industry standard questions, to qualify for as many positions as possible with a minimal input.

Students are also in full control of the image that they present to recruiters, by being able to control what makes themselves as marketable as possible. By using LinkUpWith.Me, instead of having to rely on word of mouth, students can trust that we’ll send exactly what they want us to opportunities that they haven’t even considered. By using our powerful search functions, they will be able to search for opportunities that aren’t traditionally found through just school career centers.

Students can look forward to a social community specifically focused around recruitment, and receive live statistics about expectations and match percentages to find their ideal role for the future. By making the power of big data analytics available to students in a simple, interactive event calendar, students can realistically move forwards into the job market with the path to their goals clearly laid out for them.
